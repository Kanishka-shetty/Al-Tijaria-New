Why Al Tijaria?

High Performance indicators for the company are as illustrated below:

  • High growth rate in assets value: from KD 135 million in 2000 to KD 535 million in 2019 (296%).
  • High growth in Shareholder’s equity: from KD 123 million in 2000 to KD 286 million in 2019 (132.5%).
  • High growth in operational profits: from KD 3.2 million in 2000 to KD 20.2 million in 2019 (531.3%).
  • Continued profits distribution since 2002: KD 326.5 million (≈1,070 Million USD).
  • Strong debt-to-equity and capital ratios among Kuwait’s listed firms.
  • Trust of financiers: Facilities with most Islamic Banks in Kuwait.
  • Low-risk, geographically safe investment portfolio.
  • Conservative, consistently audited accounting policies.
